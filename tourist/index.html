Tourist Places

Explore some fascinating destinations in my hometown. These spots highlight Bali's history, nature, and culture.

Bajra Sandhi Monument

A monument and museum symbolizing the struggle of Balinese people. Surrounded by a park with fresh air, great for learning history and enjoying architecture.

Learn More
Bali Museum

Perfect if you love learning about history and Balinese culture. Collections include masks, traditional musical instruments, ancient artifacts, paintings, and sculptures.

Learn More
Serangan Island (Turtle Island)

A small island part of Denpasar, famous for its turtle conservation center and calmer beaches compared to more crowded tourist spots.

Learn More
Ngurah Rai Mangrove Forest

A mangrove forest in the Pemogan area with wooden walkways and observation spots. Ideal for nature lovers, photography, and escaping the city bustle.

I AM Bali 3D Interactive Art Museum

A fun and creative place with 3D-themed art installations and plenty of photo spots. Perfect for light entertainment.
