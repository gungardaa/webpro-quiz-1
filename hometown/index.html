Hometown (Denpasar)

Denpasar is the capital city of Bali, Indonesia. It's the center of administration, culture, and daily life on the island. This city blends tradition with modernity and reflects Bali's strong cultural identity in ceremonies, dances, gamelan music, and its vibrant everyday life.

Interesting Things About Denpasar:

  • It's a bustling city filled with traditional markets, temples, and cultural activities
  • Strategically located near beaches and tourist destinations, and the gateway for visitors arriving through Ngurah Rai International Airport
  • Despite being a city, Denpasar retains Bali's local atmosphere, traditional food, art, and culture are still very much alive here
